Quadcopter Quaternions


The quadcopter uses quaternions to represent its orientation in space and use it to compute angles for feedback to the motors. The problem is that quaternions are very difficult to understand on their own. We needed a better way to debug the parts of our code that involved quaternions, so we found quaternions.online and adapted their quaternion viewer to our project. On this page, you can use our adapted viewer to monitor the orientation of the quadcopter and other calculated quaternions in real time.

Click and drag to adjust the viewport. Try out various buttons at the bottom to see what they do!
